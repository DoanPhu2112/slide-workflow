# Slide Design Rules

## Philosophy

Slides communicate ideas visually.

Text is secondary.

Every slide must contain:

- One core message
- One visual primitive
- Minimal text

---

## Text Rules

Title:

- max 8 words

Bullet:

- max 5 bullets

Bullet:

- max 8 words each

Avoid paragraphs.

---

## Layout Rules

20% title

60% visualization

20% notes

---

## Visual Rules

Prefer:

- Timeline
- Sequence
- Architecture
- Comparison

Avoid:

- Large text blocks
- Tables > 5 rows

---

## Theme Rules

Background:

- White

Primary:

- #2563EB

Font:

- GT Planar
- Inter

- 36px

Body:

- 20px
